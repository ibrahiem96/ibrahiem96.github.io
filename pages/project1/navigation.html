Discussion on Navigation

Navigation within the the game can be done in two ways. One through VR headset and controls. And the other through WASD and mouse (and perhaps keys for other controls).

With the VR wand navigation was easy as long as you got used to the controls. I did realize after making my project that perhaps certain objects could have been resized to that I could interact better with them. However, I also wanted to use up the given space appropriately.

With the WASD controls, movement was difficult, and rotating your vision was difficult--EXTREMELY difficult if you don’t own a mouse and are moving with your laptop trackpad. The one good thing about it is the WASD controls allow smooth movement. Unlike the wand’s teleportation through which if you move you can easily skip over things, the WASD allows you to move at a pace where you can see everything as you move.
