How to use

        To install simply unzip the file and then open unity and open the project folder and run the scene.

To walk around you can use the VR tools or WASD. Controls are pretty much the same. Hitting the L key will toggle the lights on and off.

Walking near certain objects will trigger noises.

Certain objects can be interacted with.

Description

        This office space takes on a new look with many different objects inside. In the front you have a projector, tv, and white board along with a meeting table and any essential meeting tools. In the corner there’s a plant and on the side there's a large stress ball. Further down is a large lava lamp in the middle of the room and tables for working. Further down is a recreational area with bean bag, laptop, standing computer desk, and of course the company title. This looks very much like a small startup office.
